## DESCRIPTION
FoodAndFuelPro is a unified operations platform for businesses managing both food service and fuel logistics — covering cost tracking, order coordination, and delivery management in one place.

**The gap we're filling:** Companies operating across food service and fuel (fleet operators, roadside services, food logistics firms) have no unified tool. They use separate systems that don't talk to each other. We're building the bridge.

## FEATURES (Planned)
- Fleet fuel cost tracking and analytics
- Food service order management and supplier integration  
- Delivery coordination across both verticals
- Multi-location support with centralized reporting
- Mobile app for field operators
